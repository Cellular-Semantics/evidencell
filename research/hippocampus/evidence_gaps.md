# Evidence gaps — hippocampus GABA interneurons

Flagged from cite-traverse (2026-04-10) and evidence extraction.
Use as seed queries for a future targeted lit search round.

## Systemically missing fields (all nodes)

- **negative_markers**: no evidence extracted for any node. The cite-traverse
  query did not prioritise negative markers. Needs a targeted query.
- **nt_type**: all 15 nodes are GABAergic — well established but no specific
  evidence items extracted because the query focused on subtype-distinguishing
  properties.

## Per-node gaps (original 9 types)

### Axo-axonic cell
- **anatomical_location**: no primary evidence for soma location (only inferred from PV basket cell papers)
- **defining_markers**: only Pvalb — no distinguishing marker separating from PV basket cell. Need papers on Unc5b, Pthlh, or other AAC-specific markers.
- **morphology_notes**: no extracted evidence (AIS targeting is the defining feature but no verbatim quote captured)

### Bistratified cell
- **anatomical_location**: soma layer not confirmed by extracted evidence
- **electrophysiology_class**: no extracted evidence

### OLM cell
- **electrophysiology_class**: no extracted evidence (theta-phase firing, regular spiking)

### Hippocampo-septal cell (P5 priority)
- **electrophysiology_class**: no extracted evidence — very thin overall (7 items, mostly anatomical)
- **colocated_types**: no evidence
- Still relies heavily on single-study characterisations

### Neurogliaform cell
- **electrophysiology_class**: late-spiking phenotype not captured
- **morphology_notes**: dense local axon / volume transmission not captured

### Ivy cell
- **anatomical_location**: soma in str. pyramidale not confirmed by extracted evidence
- **electrophysiology_class**: late-spiking not captured

### IS interneuron
- **IS-2 subtype**: absent from literature — no soma location, no specific evidence. IS-1 and IS-3 well characterised by Tyan et al. (2014).

## New types (thin evidence — 1 item each)

### Oriens-oriens (O-O) cell
- Only defining_markers (Sst, Nos1) from Chamberland et al. (2024)
- Needs: anatomical_location confirmation, electrophysiology, morphology

### Trilaminar cell
- Only anatomical_location from Katona et al. (2017)
- Needs: defining_markers (PV+/M2R+ characterisation), electrophysiology (burst firing), morphology (long-range projection)

### VIP basket cell
- Only 1 general item from Tyan et al. (2014)
- Needs: defining_markers, anatomical_location, electrophysiology, morphology (perisomatic targeting)

### R-LM cell, P-LM cell
- Only defining_markers (Sst) from Oliva et al. (2000)
- Needs: everything — single-study types, may not be distinct in modern taxonomy

### LTH cell
- Only 2 items from Hewitt et al. (2021)
- Needs: confirmation as distinct type vs SST+ subtype variant
